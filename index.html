Wood Working History


The art of woodworking began as early as the ancient Egyptians. Even then, people were fascinated by the beauty and versatility of wood. Over the years, this fascination has only grown, and woodworking has become a staple in many homes and businesses. There are many different types of woodworking, each with its own unique set of skills and techniques. Some popular forms include cabinetry, furniture making, woodturning and carpentry.
